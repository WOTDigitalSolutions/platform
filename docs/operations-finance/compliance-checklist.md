# Compliance & Legal Checklist

## Data Protection
- [ ] Draft and publish Privacy Policy aligned with GDPR & NDPR.
- [ ] Terms of Service covering service scope, liability, payment, termination.
- [ ] Data Processing Agreement template for clients using sensitive data.
- [ ] Consent capture for prayer requests and personal information.
- [ ] Document retention policy (delete/archive timelines).

## Security Controls
- [ ] Enforce MFA on Supabase, Vercel, GitHub, payment gateways.
- [ ] Role-based access control documentation (admin, moderator, viewer).
- [ ] Regular access reviews (quarterly) and credential rotation.
- [ ] Backup strategy for databases and storage (daily automated + weekly manual).
- [ ] Incident response plan (contacts, containment steps, communication template).

## Payment Compliance
- [ ] Flutterwave PCI-DSS compliance confirmation and merchant setup.
- [ ] Stripe onboarding for international transactions.
- [ ] Receipt/invoice templates with tax details where applicable.
- [ ] Chargeback dispute procedure and record-keeping.

## Accessibility
- [ ] Adhere to WCAG 2.1 AA standards in design system.
- [ ] Provide transcripts/captions for video content where possible.
- [ ] Ensure color contrast and keyboard navigation in UI.

## Contracting
- [ ] Master Services Agreement (MSA) with scope, IP ownership, confidentiality.
- [ ] Statement of Work (SOW) templates for each package tier.
- [ ] Maintenance agreement outlining SLAs, response times, fees.
- [ ] Contractor agreements with NDA/IP clauses (for designers, videographers).

## Regional Considerations
- [ ] NDPR registration (if required) for Nigerian data processing.
- [ ] Review local ministry regulations for storing congregant data.
- [ ] Align with cross-border data transfer rules when hosting outside home country.

## Governance
- [ ] Appoint data protection lead (could be founder initially).
- [ ] Maintain compliance log with audits, incidents, remediation actions.
- [ ] Annual legal review to update documents and policies.
