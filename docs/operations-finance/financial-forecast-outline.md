# Financial Forecast Template Outline

## Time Horizon
- Month-by-month view for first 12 months.
- Separate sheet for Week 1â€“18 execution to track near-term cash flow.

## Revenue Inputs
- Package sales forecast: units per package per month.
- Maintenance/retainer revenue by client tier.
- Add-on services (AI summaries, media management, workshops).
- Upsell probability metrics (e.g., 30% of Starter clients upgrade in 6 months).

## Cost Inputs
- Direct labor: Claude (engineering hours), design/video contractors, support staff.
- Tools & infrastructure: Vercel, Supabase, domain, email services, analytics, design subscriptions.
- AI usage: Claude API calls, monitoring budgets with buffer.
- Marketing spend: ads, printing, webinar hosting tools.
- Miscellaneous: legal templates, accounting, contingency (5%).

## Cash Flow Tracking
- Invoice schedule per project with expected payment dates.
- Operating expenses calendar (monthly/annual).
- Net cash position per month; trigger alerts when buffer <2 months.

## KPIs
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) & Annual Run Rate.
- Gross margin %, contribution margin per package.
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) vs Lifetime Value (LTV).
- Average implementation time, utilization rate.

## Scenario Planning
- Base case, conservative (50% of lead targets), aggressive (150%).
- What-if toggles for AI cost increases, delayed payments, higher churn.
- Runway calculation for each scenario.

## Reporting Cadence
- Monthly finance review meeting (Week 4).
- Quarterly board/update deck summarizing performance, variance vs plan.
- Update forecast after each signed contract or material change.

## Tooling Recommendations
- Start in Google Sheets or Notion Database with formulas.
- Consider migrating to a finance SaaS (e.g., Brixx, LivePlan) once revenue stabilizes.
