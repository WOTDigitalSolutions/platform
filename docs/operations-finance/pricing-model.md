# Pricing Model & Revenue Strategy

## Core Packages
| Package | Base Price (USD) | Delivery Timeline | Included Support |
|---------|------------------|--------------------|------------------|
| Starter Site | $180 (range $120–$250) | 7–10 days | 30-day post-launch support |
| Ministry Hub | $480 (range $350–$600) | 14–21 days | 60-day support + monthly report |
| Apostolic Network Portal | $850 (range $700–$1,200) | 30–45 days | 90-day support + quarterly review |

> Pricing ranges allow for complexity adjustments (custom modules, multilingual, integrations).

## Add-On Services
- AI Sermon Summaries & Tagging: $30/mo (per 20 sermons).
- Media Management & Uploads: $40/mo (weekly update cadence).
- Priority Support Upgrade: $25/mo (Starter), $40/mo (Hub).
- Onsite or virtual workshop: $150/session.
- Custom integration (per scope): starting at $200.

## Payment Terms
- Standard: 60% upfront, 40% on handover (Starter); 50/30/20 milestone for Hub & Network.
- Accept Flutterwave, direct bank transfer, Stripe (USD clients).
- Offer early payment incentive (5% discount for full upfront).

## Discount & Promotion Strategy
- Foundation Partner (first 5 clients): 15% off base price + testimonial requirement.
- Bundle discount: 10% off when combining Portal + Maintenance + AI add-on.
- Referral bonus: $50 credit or maintenance month free for each successful referral.

## Profitability Targets
- Target gross margin ≥55% (labor cap: 30%, tools: 10%, contingency: 5%).
- Add-on services to contribute 25% of monthly recurring revenue by Month 6.
- Break-even monthly load: 2 Starter + 1 Hub OR 1 Network + 1 maintenance plan.

## Review Cadence
- Quarterly pricing review based on market feedback and feature scope.
- Track conversion rates by price point to validate elasticity.
- Monitor AI usage costs (Claude API) to ensure add-on pricing covers consumption.
