# Privacy Policy - WOT Digital Solutions

**Last Updated:** November 6, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 6, 2025

---

## 1. Introduction

WOT Digital Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates websites and digital platforms for Christian ministries. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

**Please read this privacy policy carefully.** If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide
We may collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide to us:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number
- **Ministry Information:** Church/ministry name, address, role
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through third-party payment processors (Flutterwave, Stripe)
- **User Content:** Sermons, images, documents, prayer requests, attendance reports

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our websites, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent, links clicked
- **Cookies:** See "Cookies and Tracking" section below

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Services:** Build and maintain your ministry website/platform
- **Process Payments:** Handle deposits and final payments
- **Communicate:** Send project updates, support responses, invoices
- **Improve Services:** Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, add features
- **Comply with Law:** Meet legal obligations, prevent fraud

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

### Service Providers
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication (GDPR compliant)
- **Vercel:** Website hosting
- **Cloudinary:** Media storage (images, videos)
- **Flutterwave/Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Google Analytics:** Website analytics (anonymized)
- **Anthropic (Claude AI):** Sermon summarization (no personal data sent)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

### Business Transfers
If WOT Digital Solutions is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption:** HTTPS/TLS for data in transit, encryption at rest for database
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, strong passwords, two-factor authentication
- **Regular Backups:** Daily automated backups (stored securely)
- **Security Audits:** Regular reviews and updates

**However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

- Your account is active
- Needed to provide services
- Required by law (tax records: 7 years)

**Upon request, we will delete your data** (subject to legal obligations).

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### General Rights (All Users)
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing emails

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests

### NDPR Rights (Nigerian Users)
- **Consent Withdrawal:** Withdraw consent for data processing
- **Lodge Complaints:** Contact Nigeria Data Protection Commission

**To exercise your rights, contact:** wotdigitalsolutions@gmail.com

## 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Remember Your Preferences:** Login status, language
- **Analyze Usage:** Google Analytics (anonymized)
- **Improve Performance:** Caching, load times

### Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for site functionality (cannot be disabled)
- **Analytics Cookies:** Google Analytics (can be disabled via browser)

### Cookie Management
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

**Learn more:** [aboutcookies.org](https://www.aboutcookies.org)
