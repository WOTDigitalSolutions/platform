# Editorial Calendar (First 12 Weeks)

## Publishing Rhythm
- **Blog:** Biweekly (Tuesday Week 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11).
- **Newsletter:** Monthly (Week 4, 8, 12).
- **Social Thought Leadership:** Weekly micro-post aligned with campaign theme.

## Topics & Angles
- **Week 1 Blog:** “Why Ministries Need Purpose-Built Digital Systems” – include KPIs and testimonials.
- **Week 3 Blog:** “How AI Summaries Save Pastoral Prep Time” – highlight Claude integration.
- **Week 5 Blog:** “From WhatsApp Chaos to Unified Ministry Hub” – showcase workflow transformation.
- **Week 7 Blog:** “Data Stewardship for Apostolic Networks” – compliance and governance focus.
- **Week 9 Blog:** “Stories from the Field: Launching our First Foundation Partners.”
- **Week 11 Blog:** “Scaling Your Ministry with Digital Discipleship Tools.”

## Newsletter Structure
1. Founder’s Note (personal insight + scripture).
2. Feature Highlight (new capability or use case).
3. Client Spotlight/Testimonial.
4. Tips & Resources (workflow hack, template, upcoming event).
5. CTA (book call, join webinar, upgrade maintenance).

## Supporting Content
- Create social snippets (quotes, stats, audiograms) from each blog.
- Align newsletter CTAs with campaign calendar (webinar, offer, testimonial request).
- Repurpose demo clips into reels/shorts for weekly engagement.

## Workflow
- Maintain editorial board in Notion with status (Ideation → Draft → Review → Published).
- Designate reviewer for theological accuracy and brand tone.
- Track performance metrics (opens, clicks, time on page) to refine topics.
