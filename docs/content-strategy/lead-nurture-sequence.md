# Lead Nurture Email Sequence

## Email 0 – Instant Welcome (Send Immediately)
- Subject: “Welcome to WOT Digital Solutions”
- Content: Thank them for interest, reiterate mission, link to demo request, share free resource (e.g., Ministry Digital Checklist).

## Email 1 – Value Story (Day 3)
- Subject: “How Ministries Are Reclaiming Time with Smart Systems”
- Content: Story of an emerging minister (persona) moving from manual processes to Starter Site; include CTA to schedule vision call.

## Email 2 – Social Proof (Day 7)
- Subject: “See the Impact in Real Ministries”
- Content: Mini case study/testimonial, before/after metrics, screenshot; highlight Foundation Partner slots.

## Email 3 – Offer & Objections (Day 14)
- Subject: “Ready to Launch Your Ministry Hub?”
- Content: Address top objections (budget, tech readiness, data security); share payment plans and support tiers; CTA to reply with questions.

## Email 4 – Reminder & Upsell (Day 21)
- Subject: “Final Reminder: Build Your Ministry Portal with Us”
- Content: Recap key benefits, remind of deadline for launch pricing; introduce maintenance/AI add-ons; invite to webinar or live Q&A.

## Automation Notes
- Stop sequence if lead books a call or becomes a client; shift to onboarding communications.
- Track opens/clicks to segment engaged leads; send tailored follow-ups (e.g., advanced features, network case study).
- Integrate with CRM to log sequence completion and next actions.

## Content Tone
- Pastoral, encouraging, results-focused.
- Blend scripture encouragement with practical outcomes when appropriate.
- Keep emails concise (≈250 words) with a single CTA each.
