## Phone Call Script

### Opening (30 seconds)

"Hello, this is Rev. Premma from WOT Digital Solutions. Is this a good time for a quick chat?"

**If YES:**
"Great! I wanted to reach out because we help ministries like [Church Name] build professional websites and digital platforms ‚Äì without the high costs or long wait times. Do you currently have a website or any digital tools for your ministry?"

**If NO:**
"No problem! When would be a better time for me to call back? I'll only need 10-15 minutes."

---

### Discovery Questions (2-3 minutes)

1. "Do you currently have a church website? If so, how's it working for you?"
   - *Listen for pain points: outdated, hard to update, expensive to maintain*

2. "How do you currently handle things like sermon distribution, prayer requests, or online giving?"
   - *Listen for: scattered tools, manual processes, frustration*

3. "If you have a team or multiple branches, how do you collect reports (attendance, outreach, etc.)?"
   - *Listen for: WhatsApp chaos, Excel spreadsheets, no visibility*

4. "If you could wave a magic wand and have the perfect digital setup, what would it include?"
   - *Listen for: their dream solution, budget hints, timeline*

---

### Pitch (2-3 minutes)

"Based on what you shared, I think [Package Name] would be a great fit. Let me explain what's included..."

**For Starter Site:**
"You'd get a 5-page website with sermon uploads, online giving through Flutterwave, and contact/prayer request forms. Everything mobile-responsive. We'd have you launched in 7-10 days for $200."

**For Ministry Hub:**
"On top of a website, you'd get a secure team portal where your leaders can submit reports, access resources, and you'd have an admin dashboard to see everything. We even include AI-powered summaries. $500, delivered in 10-14 days."

**For Network Portal:**
"For a network like yours, you need branch-level isolation, network-wide dashboards, and role-based access. You'd see all your branches in one place, with automated monthly summaries. $1,000, delivered in 3-4 weeks."

---

### Handling Objections

**Objection 1: "That's too expensive."**

**Response:**
"I totally understand budget is a concern. Can I ask ‚Äì what were you expecting to invest? Also, compared to agencies charging $5,000-10,000, we're actually 10x more affordable. Plus, it's a one-time payment (no monthly subscriptions). And right now, we're offering Foundation Partner pricing at 20% off for our first 5 clients."

---

**Objection 2: "I need to think about it / pray about it."**

**Response:**
"Absolutely, that's wise! This is an important decision. Can I ask ‚Äì what specific questions or concerns do you have that I can help clarify? I want to make sure you have all the info you need to make the best decision for [Church Name]."

**Follow-up:**
"How about this ‚Äì I'll send you a proposal via email with all the details. You take your time to review and pray, and I'll follow up in a week. Sound good?"

---

**Objection 3: "I already have a website / We're working with someone else."**

**Response:**
"That's great! How's it working for you? Are there any frustrations or features you wish it had?"

**If they're happy:**
"That's awesome ‚Äì I'm glad you found a solution that works! Keep me in mind if you ever need upgrades or team features. And if you know any other ministers who need a site, I'd appreciate the referral!"

**If they have frustrations:**
"I hear you. A lot of ministries face similar issues. Would you be open to a quick demo of what we offer? No obligation ‚Äì just to see if there's a better fit."

---

**Objection 4: "I'm not tech-savvy / I don't know how to manage a website."**

**Response:**
"You're not alone ‚Äì most of our clients say the same thing! That's exactly why we built it simple. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use our platform. Everything is point-and-click, and we include 1-hour training. Plus, free support for the first 1-3 months (depending on your package). We're here to help!"

---

### Closing (1 minute)

**Ask for the sale:**
"So, does [Package Name] sound like a good fit for [Church Name]? If so, I can send you a proposal today and we can get started this week!"

**If YES:**
"Awesome! I'll send the proposal to [email] within the next hour. It'll include all the details, timeline, and payment info. We'll schedule a kickoff call once you're ready to move forward. Sound good?"

**If MAYBE:**
"No problem! How about I send you the proposal anyway so you have it for reference? You can review it, pray about it, and let me know when you're ready. I'll follow up in a week. Fair enough?"

**If NO:**
"I understand ‚Äì timing might not be right. Can I keep your contact and check back in 3-6 months? Sometimes needs change. And if you ever need anything or know someone who does, feel free to reach out!"

---

### Call Wrap-Up

"Thank you so much for your time today, Pastor [Name]. I pray blessings over [Church Name] and the work you're doing. I'll be in touch soon!"

**Hang up, then immediately:**
- Send proposal email (if promised)
- Add to CRM/spreadsheet (contact info, interest level, follow-up date)
- Set reminder for follow-up
