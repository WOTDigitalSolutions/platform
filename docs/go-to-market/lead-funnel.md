# Lead Funnel & CRM Flow

## Funnel Stages
1. **Lead Captured** – Via landing page form, WhatsApp message, social DM, or event signup. Auto-tag source.
2. **Discovery Scheduled** – Vision call booked (Calendly/Google Calendar) with reminder automation.
3. **Proposal Sent** – Custom PDF delivered; follow-up reminder set for 48 hours.
4. **Contract Signed** – Signed agreement + initial payment logged; onboard checklist triggered.
5. **Portal In Progress** – Project moved to active board; weekly client updates scheduled.
6. **Portal Live** – Handover complete; training session executed; testimonial request queued.
7. **Maintenance/Expansion** – Recurring support cadence, upsell opportunities tracked.

## CRM Requirements
- Tool: Notion CRM board or lightweight SaaS (e.g., Trello + Google Sheets integration).
- Fields: Lead name, ministry, contact info, package interest, channel source, stage, owner, next action, priority, notes, follow-up date.
- Automations: Reminder emails/WhatsApp, testimonial request triggered 7 days post-launch, renewal reminders 14 days before maintenance expiry.

## Intake Form Fields
- Name & ministry
- Role (Pastor, Team Lead, Network Overseer)
- Current digital tools (+ pain points)
- Desired go-live date
- Preferred package (Starter/Hub/Network/Unsure)
- Consent checkbox for follow-up communications

## Lead Scoring (1–5)
- **Fit:** Alignment with tier (2 pts).
- **Urgency:** Launch timeline <30 days (1 pt).
- **Budget readiness:** Clear willingness to invest (1 pt).
- **Engagement:** Response time, resource sharing (1 pt).

## Handover to Delivery
- Upon contract, automatically create project card in `Phase 1` board with link to onboarding checklist.
- Claude receives summarized brief (persona, package, special requirements, deadline).
- Set up shared folder for content uploads; send client instructions.

## Reporting
- Weekly dashboard: leads generated, conversion per channel, proposals outstanding, revenue forecast.
- Monthly retrospective: track lead velocity, identify nurture gaps, adjust campaigns accordingly.
