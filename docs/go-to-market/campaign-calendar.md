# 6-Week Launch Campaign Calendar

| Week | Theme | Objective | Channels | Key Assets |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|------------|
| 1 | Vision Teaser | Build awareness and curiosity | WhatsApp broadcast, Facebook story, Instagram reel | Founder intro video, teaser graphic “Digital Tools for Spiritual Impact” |
| 2 | Starter Site Spotlight | Convert emerging ministers | WhatsApp carousel, Facebook group post, Email newsletter | Landing page preview, limited-time price banner, CTA to book vision call |
| 3 | Ministry Hub Deep Dive | Engage team leaders | Facebook Live/Q&A, Telegram pinned post, Blog article | 15-min live demo, Hub feature checklist, testimonial quote (once available) |
| 4 | Proof & Testimonials | Social proof for hesitant leads | WhatsApp voice note follow-up, Instagram testimonials, Case study PDF | Client story snapshots, before/after metrics, screenshot montage |
| 5 | Network Portal Webinar | Attract network apostles | Zoom webinar/invite, LinkedIn outreach, Telegram reminder | Registration landing section, agenda slide deck, follow-up recap |
| 6 | Maintenance & AI Upsell | Lock in recurring revenue | Email nurture, WhatsApp broadcast, Blog | AI summary explainer, support tiers infographic, calendar link for advisory call |

## Rhythm & Cadence
- **Mondays:** Announce weekly theme and CTA.
- **Wednesdays:** Midweek engagement touchpoint (live, Q&A, or behind-the-scenes).
- **Fridays:** Recap and direct response push with booking/reminder.

## Metric Targets
- WhatsApp broadcast CTR ≥ 25%.
- Social engagement lift ≥ 15% week over week.
- 10 booked vision calls by end of Week 6.
- 3 signed contracts (mix of Starter/Hub) within first cycle.

## Content Production Checklist
- Maintain approval workflow for copy/graphics one week ahead.
- Capture audience responses and FAQs; feed into sales enablement scripts.
- Tag campaigns in CRM to attribute leads back to specific pushes.
