# Admin Training Plan (60 Minutes)

## Session Overview
- Duration: 60 minutes (record call for future reference).
- Format: Live video session with screen sharing.
- Participants: Lead pastor/minister, admin/staff, departmental heads as needed.

## Agenda
1. **Welcome & Objectives (5 min)**
   - Recap portal goals and expected outcomes.
   - Confirm session recording and Q&A format.

2. **Portal Orientation (10 min)**
   - Navigation overview (menus, dashboards, user profile).
   - Explain role-based access and permissions.

3. **Content Management (10 min)**
   - Adding/updating sermons, devotionals, and resources.
   - Media uploads (audio/video) via Supabase storage or embed links.
   - Managing categories and tags aligned with taxonomy.

4. **Reporting & Forms (10 min)**
   - Submitting attendance/outreach reports.
   - Viewing and exporting reports; syncing with Google Sheets.
   - Using AI summaries for quick insights and follow-up.

5. **Communication & Engagement (10 min)**
   - Managing announcements and notifications.
   - Reviewing analytics dashboard (visits, engagement, giving).
   - Leveraging internal messaging (Phase 3+ feature).

6. **Support & Maintenance (10 min)**
   - How to submit support requests and expected response times.
   - Scheduling content updates; understanding maintenance plan.
   - Best practices for data security and backups.

7. **Q&A + Next Steps (5 min)**
   - Open questions.
   - Schedule follow-up or advanced training if needed.
   - Share quick-start PDF and contact details.

## Materials & Resources
- Slide deck highlighting key features and checklists.
- Quick-start guide PDF tailored to client package.
- Short how-to clips (recorded) stored in knowledge base.

## Follow-Up
- Send recording link and slides within 24 hours.
- Provide feedback form to gauge training effectiveness.
- Update onboarding checklist with completion status.
- Flag any additional feature requests for backlog review.
