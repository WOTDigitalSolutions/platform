# Support Service Levels & Escalation

## Overview
- Support desk hours: Monday–Saturday, 9am–7pm WAT.
- Emergency availability for critical outages (Sev-1) after hours.
- Support channels: Email, WhatsApp, in-app help (Phase 2+).

## Service Tiers
### Starter Site
- Response: within 48 business hours.
- Channels: Email + self-service knowledge base.
- Included: Bug fixes, minor content guidance, quarterly health check.
- Add-ons: Priority support ($25/mo), media management ($40/mo).

### Ministry Hub
- Response: within 24 business hours.
- Channels: Email, WhatsApp, optional scheduled check-ins.
- Included: Monthly performance report, two minor change requests/month.
- Add-ons: Dedicated office hour ($60/mo), custom automation support.

### Apostolic Network Portal
- Response: within 12 business hours (Sev-1 within 4 hours).
- Channels: Email, WhatsApp, phone escalation for critical issues.
- Included: Quarterly strategy call, proactive monitoring alerts, admin refresher training.
- Add-ons: Onsite workshop (bespoke), data migration services.

## Severity Levels
- **Sev-1 (Critical):** Portal down, payment failure, data breach risk → immediate escalation to on-call engineer (Claude).
- **Sev-2 (High):** Major feature degraded (reports unavailable, login issues) → resolution target 24h.
- **Sev-3 (Medium):** Minor bug, UI inconsistency, analytics delay → resolution target 3 business days.
- **Sev-4 (Low):** Content edits, training requests, roadmap suggestions → scheduled based on SLA tier.

## Escalation Path
1. Support intake logs ticket with severity and package tier.
2. Notify assigned engineer (Claude or delegate) via Slack/WhatsApp.
3. Communicate ETA to client within SLA window.
4. Upon resolution, document fix and update knowledge base if applicable.
5. Conduct post-incident review for Sev-1/2 within 48 hours.

## Reporting & Continuous Improvement
- Monthly support summary: ticket volume, response/resolution times, top issues.
- Trends feed into product backlog (Claude) and training updates (client success).
- Client satisfaction survey quarterly; action items tracked in operations board.
