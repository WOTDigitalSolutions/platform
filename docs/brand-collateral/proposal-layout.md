# One-Page Proposal Layout (Client-Facing)

> Printable A4 / shareable PDF optimized for WhatsApp and email.

## 1. Cover Panel
- Logo placement top-right; tagline “Making Ministry Easy Through Smart Digital Systems.”
- Headline: “Digital Discipleship Systems Built for Your Calling.”
- Contact strip: website, WhatsApp, email.

## 2. Ministry Challenge Snapshot
- Bullet “Common bottlenecks” (scattered reports, manual follow-up, outdated sites).
- Supporting stat or scripture-based inspiration.
- Mini quote from founder on serving ministers.

## 3. Our Solution in One Glance
- Three icon columns summarizing Starter Site, Ministry Hub, Network Portal.
- Emphasize custom build, AI-powered insights, and pastoral empathy.

## 4. Package Comparison Grid
- Rows: Pages/Modules, Integrations, AI Features, Timeline, Support, Investment.
- Columns: Starter Site · Ministry Hub · Apostolic Network Portal.
- Highlight add-on options with footnote (AI summaries, media management, priority support).

## 5. Delivery Timeline
- Visual timeline (Day 1–7) reflecting vision meeting → build → integrations → testing → handover.
- Callout bubble: “Average launch in 7–10 days; expedited options available.”

## 6. Testimonials & Case Study Preview
- Placeholder for two client quotes (swap in as projects go live).
- Before/after metric card once results available (attendance, giving, engagement).

## 7. Investment & Terms
- Pricing summary with installment option (50/50 or 60/40).
- Maintenance & retainer packages (Starter: on-demand, Hub: $30/mo, Network: $50/mo baseline).
- Payment options (Flutterwave, bank transfer, Stripe).

## 8. Call to Action
- “Ready to See Your Portal?” CTA with calendar QR code + WhatsApp link.
- Note about limited “Foundation Partner” pricing slots.

## 9. Footer
- Legal links (privacy, terms), registered details, support email, social handles.
- QR code linking to live demo once available.

### Production Notes
- Maintain consistent margins (12–15mm), use `Playfair Display` for headings and `Inter` for body.
- Export in both high-resolution PDF and compressed version (<7MB) for messaging apps.
- Version control the proposal template in shared drive; annotate sections that need client customization.
