# Demo Video Storyboard (60 Seconds)

## Scene 1 – Opener (0–6s)
- Visual: Logo animation over warm gradient; tagline fades in.
- Voiceover/Text: “Introducing WOT Digital Solutions — digital systems built for ministry.”
- Music: Uplifting, gentle instrumental.

## Scene 2 – Pain Points (6–15s)
- Visual: Quick cuts of WhatsApp chats, scattered spreadsheets, weary minister.
- Supers: “Scattered reports.” “Missed follow-ups.” “No central hub.”
- Voiceover: “Most ministries juggle tools that were never built for the work of the Gospel.”

## Scene 3 – Starter Site Highlight (15–27s)
- Visual: Screen capture of Starter Site homepage, sermon page, giving section.
- Callouts: “Soul-winning messaging.” “Sermon media embeds.” “10-day launch.”
- Voiceover: “Our Starter Site delivers a custom-built, ministry-first web presence in days.”

## Scene 4 – Ministry Hub Dashboard (27–39s)
- Visual: Dashboard animation showing resource library, report submission, AI summary panel.
- Callouts: “Team portal and resource library.” “Instant report tracking.” “AI-powered insights.”
- Voiceover: “The Ministry Hub centralizes your teams, content, and reports — all in one secure portal.”

## Scene 5 – Apostolic Network Ecosystem (39–48s)
- Visual: Map of multi-church network, branch dashboards, internal announcements.
- Callouts: “Multi-level access.” “Automated growth summaries.” “Network-wide clarity.”
- Voiceover: “For apostolic leaders, the Network Portal brings order to every branch.”

## Scene 6 – Testimonial & Numbers (48–54s)
- Visual: Placeholder headshot + quote overlay; metric counter (e.g., “3 ministries launched”).
- Voiceover: “Trusted by ministers who want technology that speaks their language.”

## Scene 7 – Call to Action (54–60s)
- Visual: CTA cards (Book Vision Call · See Live Demo · Join Foundation Partner list).
- Contact info, social handles, WhatsApp link.
- Voiceover: “Ready to focus on the Word, not the workflow? Schedule your vision call today.”

### Production Checklist
- Capture UI footage after Phase 1 staging environment is ready.
- Record founder or professional VO; add subtitles for social media sharing.
- Export 16:9 (web), 1:1 (Instagram feed), 9:16 (Stories/Reels) variants.
