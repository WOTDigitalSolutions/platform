# Partner Ecosystem Blueprint

## Partnership Goals
- Enhance delivery capacity (design, media, support).
- Expand market reach via ministry networks and associations.
- Increase value-add services for clients (content, training, analytics).

## Priority Partner Categories
1. **Creative & Media Teams**
   - Services: sermon video editing, photography, graphics.
   - Value: packaged media management add-on, faster content updates.
   - Action: Identify two trusted vendors; define referral fees or white-label agreements.

2. **Copywriters & Translators**
   - Services: contextual storytelling, multilingual localization.
   - Value: serve French/Portuguese-speaking ministries; ensure theological alignment.
   - Action: Build roster with sample work; set quality review process.

3. **Ministry Networks & Bible Schools**
   - Services: access to cohorts of ministers.
   - Value: bulk adoption, case studies, co-hosted webinars.
   - Action: Co-create workshops/webinars on digital discipleship.

4. **Payment Providers & Financial Advisors**
   - Services: tailored giving strategies, compliance insights.
   - Value: credibility, bundle offerings with giving optimization.
   - Action: Negotiate joint promotions with Flutterwave reps; explore church finance consultants.

5. **Technology Allies**
   - Services: integration support, co-development (e.g., AI tools, SMS gateways).
   - Value: broaden product capabilities without overextending core team.
   - Action: Set up exploratory calls with Anthropic partner managers, Termii, local telcos.

## Engagement Model
- Memorandums of understanding outlining referral terms, confidentiality, joint marketing.
- Shared calendar for co-branded events, webinars, case studies.
- Quarterly partner review to assess performance and mutual opportunities.

## Enablement Assets
- Partner brief deck (vision, offerings, case studies, pricing tiers).
- Co-branded proposal template for network-wide deals.
- Training sessions so partners understand platform capabilities and positioning.

## Tracking & Governance
- Partner CRM segment with lead attribution and commission tracking.
- Onboard partners via guideline document (brand standards, messaging).
- Conduct annual compliance review for partners handling client data.
